Travel to Japan

Japan is one of the more visited countries in the world, and it isn't surprising. This country has a lot of interesting places, locals, wildlife and food.

Best times to visit this country

If you're planning to visit this big archipelago and you want to see its characteristic pink forests, you should go when it's spring, between march and april to see the flowering of the cherry trees that some really big parks have.

But the winter its a beautiful season too. The snowy trees are really lovely and there's unique things that happen only on this time of he year.

Recommended places to go

  1. Shinjuku Gyoen (Shinjuku Imperial Garden)

    2. This national park located in Shinjuku on the Tokyo prefecture was a huge garden that belonged to the feudal lord in the Edo era(1603-1868). It's a good place to visit on any season.

    This park has different plants sections:

    • The cherry tree area
    • The azalea hill
    • The maple hill
    • The avenue of sycamore trees
    • The kid's area
    • The landscape
    • Various ponds

  2. Jigokudani Yaen Koen (Snow monkey park)

    3. This calm park situated on the North of the Nagano prefecture has an interesant wildlife: snow monkeys

    To find this animals you will need to go to a remote mountain. It may be a bit difficult but it will worth it, you will be abble to see them bathing on hot springs. A special and relaxing scene that you will surely enjoy!
